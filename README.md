📊 SaaS Subscription & Pricing AuditIdentifying Growth Opportunities through Data-Driven Insights📌 Project OverviewThis project focuses on auditing subscription tiers and identifying revenue growth opportunities for a SaaS business. By analyzing user behavior, churn trends, and unit economics, I provide actionable recommendations to optimize pricing strategy and improve customer retention.🚀 Key Business Questions AddressedRevenue Growth: What is the Month-over-Month (MoM) revenue trend?Churn Risk: Which subscription tiers are at "High Risk" (over 20%) of leaving?Unit Economics: What is the Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) across different plans?Revenue Leakage: Are there inconsistencies where paid users are not generating revenue?🛠️ SQL Techniques UsedTo ensure the analysis is both powerful and easy to maintain, I utilized:Self-Joins: For time-series analysis and MoM growth calculations.Conditional Logic (CASE WHEN): For automated risk segmentation (High/Medium/Healthy).Aggregations: To calculate Churn Rates and ARPU efficiently.Data Integrity Auditing: Identifying billing gaps and "Free" vs "Paid" conversion ratios.📁 Project StructureSubscription_Analysis.sql: Clean, optimized SQL script containing the analytical queries.Sample_Subscription_Dataset.csv: Raw dataset featuring 200+ user records with geographic and financial data.📈 Sample Insights & RecommendationsInsightRecommendationExpected ImpactHigh Basic Churn: The 'Basic' plan shows higher churn than 'Premium'.Introduce a 10% loyalty discount for annual Basic renewals.+12% RetentionRevenue Leakage: Identified users on Paid plans with $0 revenue.Audit billing integration for 'Premium' and 'Enterprise' tiers.+5% Monthly RevenueGeographic Hubs: Australia and USA represent 60% of total revenue.Localize pricing for the Indian market to capture higher volume.+20% User Acquisition
